Account Deletion Request – BeMyShipper

Effective Date: March 2026

If you wish to delete your BeMyShipper account, you may request account deletion at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the BeMyShipper mobile application.
  • Go to your Profile or Account Settings.
  • Select "Delete Account".
  • Confirm your request.

If you are unable to access your account, you may also request deletion by contacting us directly at:

Email: help@bemyshipper.com

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your personal information will be removed from active systems.
  • Certain information may be retained as required by law (such as transaction records for compliance purposes).

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at help@bemyshipper.com.
